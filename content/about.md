I'm Emmanuel. I live in Göteborg, Sweden — where the rain is a feature, not a bug.

I spend more time than I'd like to admit on programming, tinkering with side projects that may or may not see the light of day, and reading books I'll confidently recommend to people before finishing them.

<!--more-->

This blog is where I write about whatever I'm currently obsessing over. Sometimes it's software. Sometimes it's something else entirely. No schedule, no theme, no promises.

If you want to say hi, the footer has all the usual links. I'm friendlier than this page makes me sound.
